The Villa Attendant will be responsible for providing superior, personalized and professional service to the Harbourcourt Residence Club Owners and their Guests. Duties of the post will include, but will not be limited to: Clean all aspects of Owner villas including bedrooms, bathrooms, living rooms, dining rooms, corridors, kitchens, patios, balconies, elevators and stairways.
