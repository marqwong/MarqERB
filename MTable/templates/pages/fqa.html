Q: Do any of your restaurants allow smoking?
▼

A: All restaurants, bars, lounges and banquet halls (including waiting rooms and lobbies) on the premises are non-smoking.

Q: Which restaurants accept reservations?
▼

A: All the restaurants we listed are accept reservation.

Q: Do I need to reserve a restaurant in advance?
▼

A: If you already know the date and time you intend to visit us, we recommend advance reservations.

Q: Is there a dress code?
▼

A: Dress codes are different at each establishment, and we do suggest you confirm directly with the restaurant of your choice for details. Gentlemen are requested to wear a jacket in the Main Dining Room, Les Saisons.

Q: We would like to celebrate a special event. Can we book flowers or cakes?
▼

A: We welcome your requests. Please inform us when you place your reservations.

Q: Are you able to accommodate food allergies or other dietary restrictions?
▼

A: We do our very best to meet individual needs. Please inform us when you place your reservations.

Q: Do you offer vegan or halal menu?
▼

A: VEGAN:We offer vegan menus at some of our restaurants.

We can prepare dishes without alcohol, pork, or lard.

Halal meat may also be available upon request, so please contact us in advance.

Q: Do you have children's menus?
▼

A: We have no special children's menus, but please inquire when making reservations regarding options.
